Vocabulary for the LOM field 1.8

4
The largest level of granularity, e.g., a set of courses that lead to a certificate. NOTE: Level 4 objects can contain level 3 objects, or can recursively contain other level 4 objects.
3
A collection of level 2 learning objects, e.g., a course.
2
A collection of level 1 learning objects, e.g., a lesson.
1
The smallest level of aggregation, e.g., raw media data or fragments.
